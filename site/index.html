Oman Climate Atlas

Long-term heat signals across Oman, derived from Open-Meteo's ERA5-backed reanalysis and checked against Seeb station observations where available. Trends use uncertainty bands, diagnostics, and documented caveats.

Methodology — data sources, threshold definitions, uncertainty quantification, urban-heat-island caveat, what we don't yet account for.

About the 1980 cut. Pre-1980 data is drawn here for transparency but excluded from trend fits. Step-change tests show ERA5 reanalysis instability before 1950 (Δ = −1.10 °C between 1940s and 1950s, p < 1e-6 — climate doesn't move that fast) and the artifact persists, in muted form, through to 1979. The same U-shape appears in a rural inland comparator (Adam), confirming this is reanalysis behaviour, not urbanisation. See the diagnostic charts below.
